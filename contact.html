Frequently asked

Is the course really free?

Yes — all twelve lessons, the exam, and the certificate are completely free, with no account required. If it helped and you'd like to chip in, you can buy me a coffee ☕ — entirely optional.

Is my progress saved?

Your progress is stored locally in your own browser, so it stays on your device. Clearing your browser data will reset it.

Can I use this to teach others?

Absolutely — share the link with students, friends, or your team. We'd love to hear how it goes.
