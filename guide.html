Aa
Capitalization counts

When a task asks for Snacks, type it with the capital S. The checker treats uppercase and lowercase as different — snacks and Snacks are not the same. Match the words exactly as written.

Stuck? Take a peek 🔎

Every lesson has a Peek at an example button. It shows the same idea using different words, so you learn the pattern without just copying the answer. Use it as often as you like — no penalty.

Totally optional. Want a challenge and extra XP? Open the bonus, solve a slightly trickier version, and claim your reward. Skip it anytime — it never blocks your progress.

A one-question quiz to lock in what you learned. Answer it correctly to finish the lesson, earn a badge, and unlock the next step. Pick the wrong one? Just try again.
