Last updated: July 1, 2026

We aim to collect as little as possible.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the Site. These tools may collect information such as pages viewed and time on site, in aggregate, to help us improve the experience.

How we use information

  • To display and measure advertising, where applicable.

Your choices

You can clear your saved progress at any time by clearing your browser's site data. You can manage cookies through your browser settings and opt out of personalized ads using the links above.
